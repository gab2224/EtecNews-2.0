Sobre Nossa Escola

A Etec

The Escola Técnica Estadual Centro Paula Souza Unidade Lins , had the purpose of implantation to generate qualified and specialized labor to serve the regional market (Lins, Guaiçara, Getulina, Sabino, Promissão, Avanhandava, Penápolis, Barbosa, Alto Alegre, José Bonifácio, Ubarana, Cafelândia, Guarantã, Guaimbê, Júlio Mesquita, Uru and Pongaí), comprising a population of around 300 thousand inhabitants. With the publication of the Secretariat of Education that would no longer maintain the Technical courses linked to that Department, as it was not part of the objectives assigned to it, there was a need to meet the aspirations of the population that claimed for the opportunity of free and quality education. In 1998, more precisely in the month of August, it was implemented as a Decentralized Class at ETEC Prof. Helcy Moreira Martins Aguiar, in the municipality of Cafelândia. with an agreement with Centro Paula Souza and the Department of Education and two classes of the Technical Course in Administration were started, one during the day and the other at night, operating in rooms borrowed from the EE Fernando Costa school in the city of Lins.

As a matter of fact

The original idea would be that all the technical courses held by the EE Fernando Costa school would be transferred to the Paula Souza Center, as long as it met the demand and there were compatible laboratories for their maintenance. In the first semester of 1999, a group of the Technical Course in Buildings and another of the Nursing Technicians began, which remain until the present date. There was a prior agreement that in the 2nd semester of 1999, the Technical Courses in Mechanics, Electrotechnics and Electronics, would also integrate the courses offered by the Paula Souza Center in Lins; however, for reasons beyond our knowledge, the teachers of the aforementioned courses chose to remain linked to the Department of Education, although, knowing that in the future they would be extinct. As there was no such transfer of courses, the Paula Souza Center started to offer the Qualification Course of Nursing Assistant, with a duration of one year. Totaling

Totaling around 360 students, always aiming at improving professional qualifications, in order to offer increasingly qualified professionals to face the competitive job market. In the 1st half of 2004, meeting the need for professional qualification of its employees, through an agreement with the Bertin Group, another course was implemented, the Meat Processing Technician, which totals around 400 students. Through State Decree nº 46.960, of July 29, 2002, published in the DOE of 07/30/2002, in its menu, it states: "It authorizes the State Treasury to allow the use, on a precarious basis, in favor of the State de Educação Tecnológica Paula Souza - CEETEPS, of part of the property that it specifies, located in the municipality of Lins", part of the property where the EE Fernando Costa operates, aiming at the implementation and maintenance of professional education mid-level technique. The School had its operating authorization published on June 16, 2006 and started to operate in its own building, renovated and adapted to the needs of the School Unit, becoming call State Technical School of Lins - ETEC of Lins. As of the 1st semester of 2007, the Computer Technician course started to be offered at the E.U., in the afternoon. High School started in the 1st semester 2008. In the 2nd semester of the same year, there was the implementation of the Technical Course in Chemistry.
